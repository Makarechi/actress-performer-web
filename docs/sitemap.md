## Page roles

### `/`
Redirect or detect preferred language. Better: default to `/en/` unless local market strongly Slovak.

### `/en/`
Main emotional and commercial landing page.

### `/en/acting/`
Professional acting profile: showreel, credits, theatre, film, series, ads.

### `/en/voice/`
Voice samples, dubbing, languages, character/narration/commercial samples.

### `/en/music/`
Vocal, piano, live performance.

### `/en/hosting/`
Event host / presenter profile.

### `/en/skills/`
Special skills: languages, aerial gymnastics, drawing, translation, piano, vocal.

### `/en/gallery/`
Curated photos, not a dump.

### `/en/casting/`
Practical page for casting directors. Fast, clear, almost no decoration.

### `/en/press-kit/`
Downloadable bios, photos, CV, official links.

### `/en/contact/`
Contact details and enquiry form.

## URL principles
- URLs should be short.
- Avoid unnecessary nesting.
- Keep English slugs even for localized pages unless there is a strong SEO reason.
- Use `noindex` for private / unlisted pages.
- Do not use dates in URLs unless there is a blog/news section.
